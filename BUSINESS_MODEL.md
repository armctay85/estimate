# Business Model Canvas - EstiMate

## 1. Value Propositions

### Primary
- **CostX Killer** - 70% cheaper than incumbent ($79 vs $208/mo)
- **Real Data** - Only platform with actual Australian tender benchmarks
- **Time Savings** - Cut estimation time by 60%

### Secondary
- **Cost Validation** - Spot inflated quotes instantly
- **Data Moat** - Crowdsourced intelligence from 500+ QSs
- **Mobile First** - Work from site, not office
